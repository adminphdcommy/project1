Gotham City

Gotham City (/ˈɡɒθəm/ GOTH-əm), or simply Gotham, is a fictional city appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics, best known as the home of Batman. The city was first identified as Batman's place of residence in Batman #4 (December 1940) and has since been the primary setting of stories featuring the character.

Gotham City is traditionally depicted as being located in the state of New Jersey, in close proximity to Metropolis. Over the years, Gotham's look and atmosphere has been influenced by cities such as New York City and Chicago.

Within the DC Extended Universe, the 2016 film Suicide Squad reveals Gotham City to be located in New Jersey. Locations used as inspiration or filming locations for Gotham City in the live-action Batman films and television series have included Chicago, Detroit, Pittsburgh, Los Angeles, New Jersey, and New York City.
